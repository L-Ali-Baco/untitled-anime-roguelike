_A resilient melee fighter designed to hold the line and control crowds with sweeping strikes._

**Base Stats**

- **Health:** 120 (Highest)
- **Mobility:** Standard (1.0x Move Speed)
- **Attack Type:** Melee (Wide arcs, heavy hitstop)

**Abilities**

- **Cleave (Secondary):** A wide-sweeping melee attack that hits multiple enemies in a cone in front of the Vanguard. Offers solid crowd control.
- **Seismic Slam (Skill 1):** The Vanguard leaps into the air and crashes down, sending out a shockwave that damages and knocks back all nearby enemies.

## üî´ Gunslinger

_A precision-based ranged specialist who relies on critical hits and quick repositioning._

**Base Stats**

- **Health:** 100
- **Mobility:** Agile (1.2x Move Speed)
- **Attack Type:** Ranged (Hitscan, high precision)

**Abilities**

- **Flashbang (Secondary):** Tosses a localized explosive that stuns enemies caught in the blast radius for a brief duration.
- **Orbital Strike (Skill 2):** Calls down a massive laser from orbit after a short delay, dealing devastating damage to a targeted zone.

## üõ†Ô∏è Scrapper

_A highly mobile, hit-and-run ranged fighter that thrives on speed and setting traps._

**Base Stats**

- **Health:** 80 (Lowest)
- **Mobility:** Very Fast (1.6x Move Speed)
- **Attack Type:** Ranged (Rapid fire, auto-aim)

**Abilities**

- **Kinetic Scrubber (Passive):** As the Scrapper moves, they build up Heat. Once the Heat meter reaches 100%, their next shot will overload, dealing a 15-stud kinetic blast that applies a 20% Armor Shred debuff (enemies take 20% more damage) to all targets caught in the radius for 5 seconds. Consumes all heat and incurs a 2.5s cooldown before heat can build again.
- **Trip-Wire Spool (Skill 1):** The Scrapper anchors a spool and runs to draw a laser trip-wire across the battlefield. Pressing the skill again locks it in place. The first enemy to cross the wire detonates it in a massive 20-stud shrapnel explosion, taking heavy damage and knockback. (8s Cooldown)
- **Scavenger's Slide (Dash):** `Shift`
  Overrides the standard dash with a high-speed, 0.35s invulnerable slide. Sliding directly into or past an enemy triggers a 12-stud AOE physical knockback blast around the target, throwing enemies out of the Scrapper's path. (3s Cooldown)
